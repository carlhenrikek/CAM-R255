R255 - Advanced Topics in Machine Learning - Probabilistic Numerics

Welcome to the Advanced Machine Learning topic in Probabilistic Numerics. This year we have the benefit of having a newly released book which we will work through parts of. The aim is for us to get an idea of what this topic is and with this try and understand statistical inference in a broader sense.

You can access the book and lots of excellent material on this webpage https://www.probabilistic-numerics.org/.

Seminars

Each seminar will have the same structure, we will jointly read the chapters that are covered and two people will currate a discussion by providing a narrative for us all to discuss the material. We will be adaptive with the material, if we find that we are moving too slow or too fast we will adapt. The aim is that everyone that puts in the effort of reading the material should come away from each session have learned something new.
